Cookie Policy – DomiClear

Last updated: February 2026

DomiClear uses cookies and similar technologies (including browser local storage and device storage) to run the service at https://domiclear.app.

What we use

We use storage technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in and maintain sessions
  • Support security features and access controls
  • Provide essential app functionality
  • Support performance monitoring where enabled

Types of cookies and storage

Essential

Required for login, session management, and security. These may include browser local storage or device storage.

Performance and diagnostics (optional)

If enabled, we may use monitoring tools to improve reliability. These tools may store identifiers in browser storage to link error reports or performance data.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies or storage may prevent the app from working properly.
